## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Cimarron Toilets

Choosing the right toilet involves more than just picking a style. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you get the best Cimarron toilet for your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, measure your space. Check the "rough-in" size, which is the distance from the wall to the center of the floor drain (usually 12 inches). Decide between a round-front bowl for smaller spaces or an elongated bowl for more comfort. Also, consider who will be using the toilet. The "Comfort Height" (or chair-height) models are excellent for adults, the elderly, or those with mobility issues, as they are easier to sit on and stand up from.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Kohler Cimarron toilets offer a range of price points. A standard two-piece model might range from $300 to $500. Models with advanced features like skirted trapways, one-piece designs, or technologies like ContinuousClean will be at the higher end, potentially from $500 to $800 or more. Remember to budget for a new seat, wax ring, and supply line, as these are often not included.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
*   **Flush Technology:** Look for AquaPiston for powerful, leak-free performance or Revolution 360 for superior bowl-cleaning action.
*   **Gallons Per Flush (GPF):** Choose between 1.6 GPF for maximum flushing power or 1.28 GPF for WaterSense certified efficiency that saves water and money.
*   **Bowl Shape and Height:** Elongated vs. Round, and Standard vs. Comfort Height are the primary choices affecting comfort and space.
*   **Special Features:** Consider extras like CleanCoat surface for easier cleaning, a skirted or concealed trapway for a modern look, or ContinuousClean for automated hygiene.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Kohler is known for its high-quality vitreous china construction, which is durable, non-porous, and stain-resistant. Check the internal components as well. The AquaPiston canister design, for example, is more durable and less prone to leaks than a traditional flapper system due to having 90% less exposed seal material. A fully glazed trapway also contributes to durability by preventing clogs over the toilet's lifespan.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Investing in a toilet from a reputable brand like Kohler ensures you're getting a product backed by decades of research and development. This translates to better performance, reliability, and easier access to customer support and replacement parts if needed. Kohler's Cimarron line is a well-established collection known for its blend of classic style and modern flushing technology.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
While a toilet isn't portable, its dimensions are critical. A round-front toilet can save you 2-3 inches of precious space in a small powder room. One-piece toilets can be extremely heavy and difficult for a single person to install, whereas two-piece models are lighter and easier to handle in separate parts. Always confirm the toilet's final dimensions to ensure it won't interfere with doors, vanities, or other bathroom fixtures.

## What Is the Best Way to Choose cimarron toilets?

Choosing the best Cimarron toilet for your home can be simple if you follow a structured approach. Here's a step-by-step guide to making the right choice.

**1. Assess Your Bathroom Layout:**
Before anything else, measure your rough-in (wall to drain center). The standard is 12 inches, but older homes can vary. Then, determine if your space can accommodate a comfortable elongated bowl or if a space-saving round-front bowl is necessary.

**2. Prioritize Your Needs:**
What is most important to you?
*   **For maximum comfort and accessibility:** Choose a Comfort Height, elongated model.
*   **For water savings:** Look for a 1.28 GPF WaterSense certified toilet.
*   **For the easiest cleaning:** A one-piece model with a skirted/concealed trapway is your best bet.
*   **For superior hygiene:** Models with Revolution 360 and ContinuousClean technologies are ideal.

**3. Set Your Budget:**
Decide how much you are willing to spend. A basic, high-quality Cimarron will be more affordable, while a fully-featured, one-piece model will be a larger investment. Don't forget to account for the cost of a seat, wax ring, and potential installation fees.

**4. Compare Key Technologies:**
Understand the difference between AquaPiston (powerful, direct flush) and Revolution 360 (swirling, bowl-cleaning flush). Both are excellent, but one may better suit your preference for either raw power or sustained cleanliness.

**5. Avoid Common Mistakes:**
*   **Forgetting the Seat:** Most Kohler toilets do not include a seat. Make sure to purchase a compatible one (e.g., elongated or round-front).
*   **Ignoring a-la-carte options:** If only your bowl is broken, buying just the Cimarron bowl can save you money.
*   **Guessing the Rough-In:** Measuring is non-negotiable. A 10-inch or 14-inch rough-in requires a specific type of toilet or an offset flange.

By following these steps, you can confidently select a Cimarron toilet that will serve your household reliably for years to come.

### How do I maintain my cimarron toilets?
Maintenance is simple. For regular cleaning, use a non-abrasive cleaner and a soft brush. Avoid in-tank drop-in tablets (unless using the ContinuousClean system, which is designed for them), as they can degrade rubber seals. Models with CleanCoat surfaces only need a gentle wipe with a damp cloth. Periodically check for leaks by adding food coloring to the tank and waiting 30 minutes to see if it appears in the bowl.
