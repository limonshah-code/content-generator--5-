## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Above Ground Pool Company

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you hit the buy button, think about who will be using the pool. If you have young children, a shallower 42-inch pool might be safer. For adults wanting to swim laps, a long rectangular or oval pool is much more practical. Consider the shape of your backyard; round pools are the most structurally efficient but can waste space in corners, whereas rectangular pools fit better against fences.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Above-ground pools vary wildly in price. Entry-level frame pools (10-14ft) typically range from $300 to $600. Mid-range options with better liners and sand filters cost between $700 and $1,200. Premium large-scale sets or semi-permanent steel wall pools can go from $1,500 to over $3,000. Don't forget to budget for water, chemicals, and ground preparation materials like sand or leveling dirt.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for advanced filtration systems. Sand filters are almost always worth the extra investment over cartridge filters as they require less frequent maintenance. Check for technologies like HydroAeration, which improves water clarity. The joint design is also vital; look for composite or powder-coated joints that prevent metal-on-metal contact to stop rust before it starts.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
The liner is the most vulnerable part of the pool. Look for terms like Tritech or 3-Ply PVC, which indicate reinforced layers. For the frame, galvanized steel with inner and outer powder coating is the gold standard for rust resistance. A thicker wall or liner generally translates to a longer lifespan and fewer leaks over time.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Intex and Bestway dominate the market for a reason. They offer extensive customer support, readily available replacement parts, and widespread compatibility with third-party accessories. Buying from an established brand ensures that if a part breaks three years from now, you can actually find a replacement rather than having to scrap the whole pool.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Determine if you want to take the pool down every winter or leave it up. If you plan on moving it, look for ClickConnect or Easy Lock systems that don't require tools. Ensure you have a flat space that is at least two feet larger than the pool itself on all sides to allow for structural supports and easy access.

## Is it A Wise Decision to Buy above ground pool company?

Investing in a high-quality above-ground pool is a fantastic decision for families looking to maximize their summer enjoyment without the massive $40,000+ price tag of an in-ground installation. These pools offer a private, clean environment for your children to learn how to swim and provide a perfect hub for social gatherings.

When compared to alternatives like community pool memberships or frequent trips to the beach, an above-ground pool pays for itself within a season or two. ## What Is the Best Way to Choose above ground pool company?

Selecting the perfect pool requires a systematic approach to ensure you don't end up with a product that doesn't fit your lifestyle. Follow these steps:

1.  **Measure Your Space:** Use a tape measure to mark out the exact dimensions in your yard. Don't forget to account for the protruding legs on rectangular models.
2.  **Check Local Regulations:** Some municipalities require fences or permits for any pool over a certain depth.
3.  **Evaluate the Ground:** If your yard has a slope of more than 1-2 inches over the span of the pool, you will need to hire someone to level it or do the digging yourself. Never level by adding dirt; always level by digging out the high side.
4.  **Prioritize the Filter:** If the pool comes with a small cartridge filter, consider budgeting for an immediate upgrade to a sand filter. It will save you hours of frustration.
5.  **Look for Bundles:** Sets that include covers, ground cloths, and maintenance kits usually offer better value than buying everything separately.

Common mistakes to avoid include setting the pool up on a deck not rated for the weight (water is very heavy!) or ignoring the importance of a ground cloth, which protects the liner from sharp rocks and roots.

## FAQ

**What is the best above ground pool company for beginners?**
Bestway is often considered the best for beginners because of their ClickConnect systems which require absolutely no tools. Their Steel Pro MAX line offers a great entry point with manageable sizes and complete kits that take the guesswork out of your first setup.

**How much should I spend on above ground pool company?**
For a reliable family pool that will last more than one season, aim to spend between $500 and $900. This range usually gets you a durable frame and a decent filtration system. Budgeting an extra $100 for a water testing kit and initial chemicals is also recommended.

**What features are most important in above ground pool company?**
The filtration system is the most critical feature. A pool is only as good as its water quality. Look for a pump with a high GPH (Gallons Per Hour) rating relative to the pool size. Puncture-resistant liners and rust-resistant frames are the next most important factors for longevity.

**How do I maintain my above ground pool company?**
Maintenance involves three pillars: circulation, cleaning, and chemistry. Run your filter at least 8-12 hours a day. Skim the surface daily and vacuum the floor weekly. Test your pH and chlorine levels 2-3 times a week to ensure the water is safe and clear.

**Are expensive above ground pool company worth it?**
Yes, higher-priced models like the Intex Ultra XTR series offer galvanized steel and sand filters that significantly reduce long-term maintenance costs and prevent rust. Cheaper models often require parts replaced more frequently, which can cost more in the long run.

**What brands make the best above ground pool company?**
Intex and Bestway are the undisputed leaders. Intex is known for its high-end Ultra XTR frame technology and sand filters, while Bestway excels in innovative designs like the Swim Vista windows and the APX 365 year-round pools.

**How long do above ground pool company typically last?**
A well-maintained frame pool usually lasts between 5 and 8 years. Semi-permanent steel wall pools, like the Bestway Hydrium, can last 10 to 15 years if the liner is replaced every few seasons and the frame is kept free of major rust.

**Can I find good above ground pool company on a budget?**
Absolutely. Round pools in the 10ft to 14ft range offer the best value. They use less material for the frame and require smaller pumps, allowing you to get a high-quality liner and frame for a much lower price point than rectangular models.
