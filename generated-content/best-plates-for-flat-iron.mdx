## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Plates For Flat Iron

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
The first step in choosing the best plates for flat iron is identifying your hair type and styling goals. If you have thick, coarse hair, you will likely benefit from wider plates and titanium materials that can handle the volume.
