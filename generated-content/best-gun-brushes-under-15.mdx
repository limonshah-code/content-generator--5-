## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Gun Brushes Under 15

Navigating the world of affordable gun cleaning supplies can be tricky. A low price tag is appealing, but not if the tool fails after one use. Here’s a guide to help you select high-quality, effective gun brushes that fit your budget.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First and foremost, identify the firearms you need to clean. Brushes are not one-size-fits-all. Bore brushes must match the specific caliber of your barrel (e.g., 9mm, .223, 12-gauge). Utility brushes, while more versatile, come in different materials. Bronze or brass bristles are for heavy-duty scrubbing on internal metal parts, while nylon is the go-to for general purpose cleaning and protecting delicate exterior finishes.
