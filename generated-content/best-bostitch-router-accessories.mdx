## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Bostitch Router Accessories

Choosing the right router accessories can dramatically expand your tool's capabilities. Before you make a purchase, consider these key factors to ensure you get the best performance and value for your specific needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First and foremost, confirm that the accessory is compatible with your specific router model. Brands often use proprietary mounting systems, so a DEWALT guide may not fit a Bostitch or Bosch router without an adapter. Think about the type of work you do most often. If you frequently cut dadoes, an edge guide is essential. For decorative work or joinery, a set of template guide bushings is a must-have.
