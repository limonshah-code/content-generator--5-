## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Bathtub Non Slip Stickers

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase, determine the surface type of your bathtub. Most stickers work best on smooth, non-textured surfaces. If you have a tub with built-in texture, the adhesive might not bond correctly.
