Before you start shopping, understand your scalp. Is it oily, dry, sensitive, or prone to dandruff? Do you have fine hair that gets weighed down easily, or thick, textured hair that needs intense moisture? Your answers will guide your choice. What is the main problem you want to solve? Are you looking for daily refreshment, instant itch relief for braids, a deep cleanse for product buildup, or a potent anti-dandruff solution? Be specific. This is a critical step. Look for high-quality peppermint oil (Mentha piperita) high on the ingredient list.
