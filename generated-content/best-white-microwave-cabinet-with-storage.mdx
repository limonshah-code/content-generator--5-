## What to Look for Before Buying the Best White Microwave Cabinet With Storage

Choosing the right microwave cabinet involves more than just picking a style you like. To ensure you get a piece of furniture that is functional, durable, and fits your space perfectly, consider these key factors before making a purchase.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, measure the microwave you plan to place in the cabinet. Check its width, depth, and height, and compare these to the dimensions of the open shelf or countertop space on the cabinet. Ensure there is enough clearance around the microwave for proper ventilation, typically a few inches on each side and the back. Think about what else you need to store. Do you need deep drawers for pots, cupboards for dinnerware, or open shelves for cookbooks? Your specific storage needs will determine the ideal configuration.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($100-$350 ranges)
White microwave cabinets come in a wide price range, generally from around $100 for basic models to over $350 for larger, more feature-rich units. Simpler, more compact cabinets made from particleboard will be at the lower end.
