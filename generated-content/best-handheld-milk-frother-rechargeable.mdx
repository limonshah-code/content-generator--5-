## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Handheld Milk Frother Rechargeable

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before purchasing, consider what liquids you will be frothing most often. If you exclusively use whole dairy milk, almost any frother will work. However, if you prefer oat, almond, or soy milk, you should look for a model with a higher RPM or a double-spring whisk, as non-dairy milks require more energy to create stable foam. Also, consider if you need a frother for tasks beyond coffee, such as mixing protein shakes or whipping eggs, which might necessitate a model with multiple detachable heads.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Handheld rechargeable frothers typically fall into three price brackets. The $10-$15 range covers basic, single-speed models that are reliable but may lack extra features. The $18-$30 range is the 'sweet spot' where you find multi-speed settings, LED indicators, and higher-quality materials like 304 stainless steel. Above $35, you enter the premium territory with features like magnetic charging docks, high-resolution digital displays, and exceptionally large batteries.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Focus on three main specs: RPM (rotations per minute), battery capacity (mAh), and charging type. A motor that reaches at least 10,000 RPM is usually sufficient for standard foam, but 13,000 to 20,000 RPM is better for thicker creams. Look for at least a 1200mAh battery to avoid frequent charging. USB-C is the modern standard and is highly recommended over older Micro-USB ports for faster and more convenient charging.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
The rod and whisk should always be made of food-grade 304 stainless steel to prevent rust and ensure safety. Check for the 'wobble' factor; higher-quality frothers are better balanced and will not vibrate excessively in your hand at high speeds. A well-built motor should sound smooth and not grinding. Additionally, look for brands that offer at least a 1-year warranty, as these small motors can sometimes burn out with heavy use.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
