## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Blankets For Night Sweats

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Determining how you intend to use the blanket is the first step. If you are looking for something to cover your existing bedding, a thin cooling throw might be best. However, if you want a complete replacement for your heavy quilt, look for a cooling comforter with a down-alternative fill. Consider whether you need a reversible option that can also provide warmth during the colder months, or a dedicated summer blanket that is ultra-thin for maximum airflow.

Since night sweats require frequent washing of bedding, durability is paramount. Look for single-needle or reinforced stitching that prevents the internal fill from clumping or shifting. Fabrics that are anti-pilling and anti-snagging will look new for longer.
