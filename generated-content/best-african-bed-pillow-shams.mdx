## What to Look for Before Buying the Best African Bed Pillow Shams

Choosing the perfect pillow shams involves more than just picking a color. To find a set that you'll love for years, consider these key factors.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about your bedroom's overall style. Are you aiming for a bohemian look, a modern minimalist vibe, or a romantic, classic feel? The shams you choose should complement your existing duvet cover, sheets, and headboard. Also, consider the size of your pillows. Shams come in standard, queen, and king sizes, so measure your pillows to ensure a snug and proper fit. A sham that is too large will look sloppy, while one that is too small won't fit at all.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($15-$50 ranges)
Pillow shams are available at a wide range of price points. You can find excellent quality microfiber shams for around $15 to $25. If you're looking for premium materials like high-thread-count Egyptian cotton or intricate designs, expect to pay between $30 and $50 or more. Set a budget before you start shopping, but remember that investing a little more in durable, high-quality materials can pay off in the long run with better comfort and longevity.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Pay close attention to the material. Microfiber is soft, durable, and affordable, while cotton is breathable and naturally hypoallergenic. The closure type is another important feature.
