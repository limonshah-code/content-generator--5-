## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 2 Cubic Feet Microwave

Choosing the right large-capacity microwave involves more than just picking the biggest one. To ensure you get an appliance that truly meets your needs, consider these critical factors before making a purchase.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, assess how you will use the microwave. Are you a large family that needs to cook or reheat big casserole dishes? Or do you primarily use it for defrosting large cuts of meat and making popcorn? A 2.0 to 2.2 cubic feet model is excellent for families and those who entertain often. Also, measure your available counter space carefully. These are large appliances, and you need to ensure you have enough room for the unit and proper ventilation around it.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Large-capacity microwaves with advanced features can range in price. You can find basic, powerful 2.0 cu. ft. models from around $150 to $250. For premium models with features like inverter technology, cyclonic wave cooking, and advanced sensors, expect to pay between $250 and $400 or more. Set a budget that aligns with the features you truly need to avoid overspending on functionalities you will never use.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
- **Wattage:** Look for at least 1100-1250 watts in a microwave this size. Higher wattage means faster, more even cooking.
- **Sensor Cooking:** This is a must-have feature. It uses sensors to detect humidity and automatically adjusts cooking time and power, preventing over- or under-cooking.
- **Inverter Technology:** Found in higher-end models, this technology delivers a continuous, controlled stream of power for more even cooking and defrosting, especially with delicate foods.
- **Turntable:** Ensure it has a large, removable turntable (14-16 inches) for even cooking and easy cleaning. Some models offer an on/off option for rectangular dishes.
