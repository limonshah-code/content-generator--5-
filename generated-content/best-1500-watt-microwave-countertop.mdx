## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 1500 Watt Microwave Countertop

Choosing the right high-power microwave involves more than just picking the one with the highest wattage. Consider these crucial factors to ensure your new appliance meets all your kitchen needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, assess how you'll use the microwave. Are you mainly reheating leftovers, defrosting meats, or cooking full meals? If you have a large family, a model with over 2.0 cubic feet of capacity is ideal. For those who love crispy food, a combination microwave-crisper like the Chefman is a perfect fit. If convenience is your priority, look for models with smart sensors, pre-programmed menus, or even voice control capabilities. Match the features to your lifestyle.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
High-power countertop microwaves can range in price from around $120 to over $300. Basic, powerful models without many bells and whistles will be on the lower end. Mid-range models ($150-$220) typically include features like sensor cooking and larger capacities.
