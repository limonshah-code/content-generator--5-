## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Drew Barrymoore Air Fryer

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before making a purchase, consider how many people you typically cook for. If you are a single person or a couple living in an apartment, the 3-quart models are excellent and save precious counter space. However, if you have a family of four or more, or if you enjoy meal prepping for the week, the 6-quart or 9-quart TriZone models are much more appropriate. Also, think about what you cook; if you want to make whole chickens or turkeys, the 9-quart model with the removable divider is the only real choice.
