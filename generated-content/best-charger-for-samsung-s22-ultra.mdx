## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Charger For Samsung S22 Ultra

Choosing the right charger is crucial to maximizing your phone's potential and ensuring its longevity. Here are the key factors to consider.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First and foremost, ensure the charger is fully compatible with the Samsung S22 Ultra. This means it must support not just USB Power Delivery (PD) but specifically the Programmable Power Supply (PPS) protocol. The S22 Ultra requires PPS to enable its 'Super Fast Charging' (25W) and 'Super Fast Charging 2.0' (45W) modes. A standard PD charger without PPS will charge much slower. Consider your use case, do you need a single charger for your nightstand, or a multi-pack for your home and office?

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
You don't need to buy the official Samsung charger to get great performance. High-quality third-party chargers are widely available. A single 25W fast charger with a cable can range from $12-$20. A more powerful 45W charger might cost between $18-$30. Value packs containing two chargers and two cables can range from $20-$40, often providing the best overall deal. Set a budget, but prioritize safety and compatibility over the absolute lowest price.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
The most important feature is the charging wattage. The S22 Ultra supports up to 45W.
