## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Cordless Vacuums For Vinyl Plank Flooring

Choosing the right cordless vacuum for your vinyl plank floors involves more than just picking the one with the most suction. To ensure you get a machine that protects your floors and meets your cleaning needs, consider these key factors.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, assess your home environment. Do you have pets that shed heavily? You'll need a vacuum with an anti-tangle brush roll. Is your home large or small? This will determine the required battery runtime. For homes with a mix of vinyl and carpet, look for a model with an adjustable head or multiple power settings.
