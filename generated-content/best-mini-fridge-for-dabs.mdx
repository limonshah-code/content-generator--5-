## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Mini Fridge For Dabs

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before making a purchase, identify how many containers you plan to store. If you only have a few jars, a 4L personal fridge is perfect. However, if you are a collector with a large variety of extracts, you should look into compressor-based models like the 24 or 48-can units. Consider where you will place it; a bedroom requires a whisper-quiet thermoelectric model, while a basement bar can handle a slightly louder compressor unit.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
You can find quality mini fridges for dabs in various price ranges. Entry-level 4L thermoelectric fridges usually fall between $35 and $60. Mid-range units with larger 6L capacity or specialized features like the Terpkooler range from $70 to $150. High-capacity compressor models designed for beverage storage can go from $180 to $300. Selecting the best model involves a few strategic steps. First, measure the space where you intend to keep the fridge to ensure it fits with proper ventilation.
