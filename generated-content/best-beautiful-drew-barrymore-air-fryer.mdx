### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
The Beautiful line is known for its sturdy construction and premium feel. Look for features that indicate durability, such as a stainless steel heating element. ### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Measure your counter space before you buy! An air fryer will likely become a permanent fixture, so ensure it fits comfortably under your cabinets. The 9-quart TriZone model has a particularly large footprint, so it requires significant dedicated space. The 3-quart models are ideal for smaller kitchens where space is at a premium. Also, consider the weight if you plan on storing the appliance in a cabinet and taking it out for each use.
