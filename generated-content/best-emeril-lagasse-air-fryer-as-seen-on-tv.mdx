### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Emeril Lagasse air fryers offer options across different price points. Compact, basket-style models can typically be found in the $100-$150 range. The more versatile, multi-functional countertop oven models, like the Everyday 360 or the 26 QT French Door, usually fall between $150 and $250. ### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for features that will make your life easier. Multiple cooking presets (air fry, bake, roast, rotisserie) add versatility. Features like 360° air circulation technology are key to achieving that perfectly crispy, even finish. For larger models, an interior light and a glass door are crucial for monitoring your food without interrupting the cooking process.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
An air fryer is an investment, so look for durable construction. Stainless steel bodies are not only stylish but also more robust and easier to clean than plastic. Reading user reviews can provide insight into the long-term durability of a specific model and its components.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers

The Emeril Lagasse line is known for its chef-quality design and construction, aiming to bring professional-grade tools to the home kitchen.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Counter space is valuable real estate in any kitchen. Before buying, measure the area where you plan to place the air fryer, and check the product dimensions. Remember to leave some clearance around the unit for ventilation. The large countertop oven models offer great capacity but have a significant footprint. If space is limited or you plan to store the appliance in a cabinet when not in use, a more compact, lightweight model like the 6-quart basket-style fryer might be a better fit.
